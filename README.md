
# Metabolic-Outcome-Estimator (Public)
A transparent, goal-driven metabolic modeling framework that evaluates the consequences of food intake beyond calories. Original concept by the founder of the LORI Framework.


## Overview

Metabolic-Outcome-Estimator is a goal-driven health modeling framework designed to evaluate the **metabolic consequences of food intake**, rather than merely counting calories.

This project introduces a structural approach to understanding how food composition, cooking methods, and nutrient distribution impact the human body relative to a defined target.

---

## Concept Positioning

> Not just a calorie counter — a metabolic consequence estimator.  
> 不只是熱量計算器，而是代謝後果評估系統。

This framework includes calorie estimation but extends further to evaluate **how intake affects metabolic outcomes**, not just numerical energy totals.

---

## Key Features

- Goal-driven evaluation (Target → Intake → Outcome)
- Cooking-adjusted estimation (boiled, fried, grilled, etc.)
- Structural separation of sugar, saturated fat, and unsaturated fat
- Transparent, explainable estimation (no black-box)
- AI-assisted intake estimation with user correction

---

## System Philosophy

This system emphasizes:

- Transparency over automation  
- Understanding over blind output  
- Human judgment over AI authority
